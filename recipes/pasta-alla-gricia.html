Pasta alla Gricia

Description

Pasta alla Gricia esentially takes Cacio e Pepe as a base and adds guanciale or pancetta to it to make pasta alla gricia.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1/2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 cup guanciale or pancetta, sliced
  • 1 cup Pecorino Romano, finely grated
  • Some black pepper

Additionally, reserve 1-2 cup of pasta water from after cooking the spaghetti.

Steps

  1. Cut guanciale or pancetta into slices.
  2. Fill a pot with water enough to cover at least half the spaghetti.
  3. Add salt to the water and get it boiling.
  4. Add your meat onto a cold pan, and set it to medium-low heat to render the fat. Let the meat crisp up and get a little browning.
  5. Once the meat is crisped up and starts browning a little, scoop it up and put it in a bowl, set aisde.
  6. Grate some black pepper into the pan with the pork fat, and stir it around a little. This is to bloom the pepper for maximum aroma.
  7. When the pasta is a few minutes from being al dente, scoop 1-2 spoonfuls of pasta into the pan. Mix with the pork fat and pepper.
  8. As soon as the pasta is al dente, reserve 1-2 cups of pasta water and trasnfer pasta from pot to pan. Mix to combine with the pork fat, black pepper, and water mixture. Add additional pasta water as needed.
  9. Once pasta is mixed to your liking, turn off the stove and add in the cheese. Mix.
  10. Add in the cooked guanciale or pancetta bits. Mix again.
  11. Plate it up.
  12. Additionally, garnish with a few cracks of fresh black pepper and some extra cheese.

Enjoy!
