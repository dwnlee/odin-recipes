Azshari Salad

Prepares 5 Azshari Salads.

Recipe Discovery

  • Zone: Dalaran (Legion)
  • NPC: Nomi
  • Discovery: Nomi's Test Kitchen

Effect

Restores 28846 health and 14423 mana over 20 sec. Must remain seated while eating. If you spend at least 10 seconds eating you will become well fed and gain 13 Haste for 1 hour.

Reagents

  • 1x Suramar Surf and Turf
  • 5x Dreamleaf
  • 5x Fjarnskaggl
  • 5x Foxflower
  • 5x Aethril

Directions

  1. Go to a kitchen in a capital city (e.g. Dalaran (Legion)).
  2. Open the professions tab and click on the "Cooking" profession.
  3. Check to see if you have all of the required reagents.
  4. Stand next to a stove, oven, or campfire.
  5. Click "Cook" (2 second cast)!
