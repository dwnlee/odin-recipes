Cacio e Pepe

Description

Cacio e pepe is a simple, yet classic Roman pasta dish. It only takes a few ingredients: pasta, cheese, and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti
  • 1 tablespoon salt
  • 1 1/2 cups of Pecorino Romano cheese, finely grated
  • 1/2 tablespoon black peppercorns, grounded

Additionally, reserve 1 cup of pasta water from after cooking the spaghetti.

Steps

  1. Fill a pot with water enough to cover at least half the spaghetti.
  2. Add salt to the water and get it boiling.
  3. In another pan, start toasting your pepper on medium-low heat.
  4. Once the pasta is about 1-2 minutes from being al dente, transfer 1-2 scoops of pasta water from the pot to the pan.
  5. Reserve 1 cup of extra pasta water.
  6. Start mixing the pepper with the water in the pan.
  7. Transfer pasta from pot to pan when al dente, mix pasta with pepper and water. Add additional pasta water as needed in little bits.
  8. Once the pasta is mixed to your liking, turn off the heat, add in the cheese and start mixing until cheese is combined and it turns into a sauce with the pasta water.
  9. Additionally, garnish with a few cracks of fresh black pepper and some grated or shredded cheese.

Enjoy!
